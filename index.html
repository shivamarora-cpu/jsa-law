Vikram Raghani is one of India's leading private equity and M&A advisors, having guided global investors and sovereign wealth funds such as Temasek, EQT, GIC, TPG, and SMBC on landmark transactions that have shaped industries and set new benchmarks.

As a member of JSA's Executive Committee and former Co-Chair of its Corporate Practice, he combines institutional leadership with deep sector expertise.

Ranked by Chambers and Partners in Band 1 for Private Equity in India and named an "A-list" lawyer by the India Business Law Journal in 2026, Vikram is recognised as a practical dealmaker known for measured judgment, strategic counsel, and precision in execution.

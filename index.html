Vikram Raghani is a member of JSA's Executive Committee and specialises in M&A, private equity, and securities laws. Before joining the Executive Committee, he served as the Co-Chair of the firm's Corporate Practice.
He advises leading Indian and international companies on a wide range of M&A and private equity transactions across listed and unlisted markets, including complex squeeze-outs. Vikram is also a trusted advisor to global investors such as Temasek, EQT, and GIC.
